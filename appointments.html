What to expect

  • Our care coordinator will call or email you within one business day to confirm your appointment time.
  • New patients are typically seen within 3–5 business days. Urgent referrals are often seen the same week.
  • Your first visit includes a 60-minute comprehensive consultation — not a rushed intake.
  • We verify your insurance before your visit — no surprise bills.
  • Parking available on-site. Wheelchair-accessible entrance on Ashford Avenue.
