New Patient Guide

1

Schedule Your Appointment

Call us at (305) 555-0192 or request an appointment online. Our scheduling team will collect basic information and confirm your insurance.

2

Complete Your Paperwork

Download and fill out the new patient forms below before your visit, or arrive 20 minutes early to complete them in office. Bring a valid photo ID and insurance card.

3

Gather Your Medical Records

Bring any relevant imaging (MRI, X-ray, CT scans), prior treatment notes, and a current list of medications. This helps Dr. Rivera build an accurate picture of your condition.

4

Your First Consultation

Your initial visit typically lasts 45–60 minutes. Dr. Rivera will review your history, conduct a physical exam, discuss your goals, and outline a personalized treatment plan.

5

Begin Your Treatment Plan

Follow-up appointments, procedures, or referrals will be coordinated by our care team. We'll keep you informed at every step and are always available to answer questions.

Office Policies

Appointment Cancellation

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule an appointment. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a $50 fee. Repeated no-shows may affect future scheduling.

Prescription Refills

Prescription refill requests require 72 hours' advance notice. Controlled substance refills are only processed during scheduled office visits and cannot be called in after hours.

Privacy & HIPAA

Your medical information is protected under HIPAA. We will not share your records without written consent except as required by law or for direct treatment purposes.

After-Hours Urgent Care

For urgent medical concerns after hours, please call our main line to be connected to our on-call service. For life-threatening emergencies, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.
